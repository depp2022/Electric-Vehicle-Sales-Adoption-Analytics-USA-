# Electric-Vehicle-Sales-Adoption-Analytics-USA-
This project analyzes over 150K electric vehicle registration records to understand EV adoption trends, manufacturer performance, geographic distribution, and market growth. It also compares multiple forecasting techniques to estimate future EV registrations.


## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze historical EV adoption trends.
- Explore the geographical distribution of EV registrations.
- Compare Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs).
- Identify the most popular manufacturers and vehicle models.
- Analyze electric range and CAFV eligibility.
- Forecast future EV registrations using multiple prediction models.

---

## 📊 Dataset

- **Source:** Kaggle – Electric Vehicle Population Data
- **Records:** 150K+ EV registrations
- **Features:** Model Year, Make, Model, Electric Vehicle Type, Electric Range, County, City, CAFV Eligibility, and more.

- CAFV eligibility analysis

---

## 📉 Forecasting Models

To estimate future EV registrations, the following forecasting techniques were implemented and compared:

- Linear Regression
- Polynomial Regression
- Exponential Curve Fitting

These models help evaluate potential EV market growth based on historical registration trends.

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- EV registrations have increased significantly over the last decade.
- Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) dominate the market compared to Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs).
- Tesla is the leading manufacturer in terms of registrations.
- Average electric range has improved considerably over recent model years.
- Forecasting models indicate continued growth in the EV market.
